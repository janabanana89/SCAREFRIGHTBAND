Meet the Band

Spencer

Bass Guitarist - Spencer is known for his deep, resonant bass lines that drive the band's sound forward. His rhythm is crucial to the band's heavy beats.

Conan

Drummer - Conan's powerful drumming provides the backbone for the band's energetic performances. His technique and stamina are unmatched.

Axel

Lead Guitarist - Axel's lightning-fast solos and melodic mastery set the tone for the band's high-energy performances. His guitar work is iconic.

Ryker

Rhythm Guitarist - Ryker's solid rhythm guitar provides the foundation for the band's heavy sound. His style complements Axel's leads perfectly.

Ezra

Keyboardist - Ezra adds depth and atmosphere to the band's music with his keyboard work. His ability to blend different sounds is a key element of their unique style.

Five friends (Spencer, Conan, Axel, Ezra, and Ryker) started a band together to entertain young audiences in the form of rock music. They started playing a few gigs at local coffee shops and slowly built their fan base to large concerts and festivals. They are journeying to expand and grow their music platform performing in different cities and tours. Finding joy and excitement in creating their music, they hope to inspire individuals to follow their paths and dreams just like they did.
